ABOUT BILLIE


Billie is a bill-splitting application created to make sharing expenses simple, fair, and stress-free. Designed especially for groups of friends, Billie helps users divide shared bills equally and transparently.


Whether it’s dining out, traveling together, or managing shared household expenses, Billie ensures that everyone pays their fair share while promoting clarity, balance, and trust among friends.


With an easy-to-use interface and a focus on fairness, Billie turns the hassle of manual bill splitting into a smooth and enjoyable experience—so users can focus more on making memories and less on doing the math.

What Billie Can Do

Create a Bill

Quickly create a bill to split among your friends, keeping everything organized and transparent.

Add Users

Invite other users to join your group and share the bill split, ensuring everyone is included.

Add Expenses

Easily add expenses to the bill and let Billie calculate each person’s fair share automatically.
